    Steve Barnes

    Beer: Juicy Jay

    Location: Leigon Brewing

    5/5
    Apperance: 5 | Taste: 5| Overall Score: 5

    Pours a translucent chestnut with a thin head. The faintest hint of lacing. Delicate musty scent, maybe a little corn and shoe leather. Excellent minty taste, accompanied by sweat and coffee. Thick and chewy mouthfeel and long, long finish.

    May 04, 2021

    Stephanie Bennett

    Beer: Coco Loco

    Location: NoDa Brewing Company

    4/5
    Apperance: 4 | Taste: 4 | Overall Score: 4

    Pours a breathtaking golden color with a nine-inch head. Amazing lacing. Lovely tart aroma, with overtones of caramel and molasses. Excellent yeasty flavor, and I also get some bacon and apple. Thick and chewy mouthfeel and light finish.

    Mar 03, 2021

    Kenny Cooper

    Beer: Good Morning Vietnam

    Location: Wooden Robot Brewery

    3/5
    Apperance: 3 | Taste: 3 | Overall Score: 3

    Pours a dense dark chocolate color with a cumulonimbus head. Beautiful lacing. Brilliant lemony scent, punctuated with orange and mango. Excellent biscuity flavor, with corn and citrus. Dense, dense mouthfeel and bitter finish.

    May 08, 2021

    Lisa Brooks

    Beer: Rainbow Sherbet

    Location: Triple C Brewing Company

    2/5
    Apperance: 2 | Taste: 2 | Overall Score: 2

    Pours a hazy greyish-green with a ruddy brown head. The faintest hint of lacing. Nightmarish medicinal nose, with overtones of circus peanut and split pea soup. Absolutely disgusting oaky taste, laced with a touch of bourbon and rosewater. Watery mouthfeel and very light finish.

    Jul 30, 2021
